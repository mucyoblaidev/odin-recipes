FIESTA SLOW COOKER SHREDDED CHICKEN TACOS

TRY THE Fiesta Slow Cooker Shredded Chicken Tacos:
This chicken tacos recipe is easy to make in a slow cooker. Spoon the filling into warm tortillas for a very tasty meal.

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 3 tablespoons taco seasoning mix
  • 1 pound skinless, boneless chicken breasts

STEPS

  1. Step 1

    2. Combine chicken broth and taco seasoning mix in a bowl.

  2. Step 2

    3. Place chicken in a slow cooker. Pour chicken broth mixture over chicken.

  3. Step 3

    4. Cook on Low for 6 to 8 hours. Shred chicken
