SOUTHERN POTATO SALAD

TRY THE S P SALAD:
This Southern potato salad includes eggs, celery, and relish and should be served warm.

INGREDIENTS

  • 4 potatoes
  • 4 large eggs
  • ½ cup mayonnaise
  • ½ stalk celery,chopped
  • ¼ sweet relish
  • 1 clove garlic,minced
  • 2 tablespoons prepared mustard
  • salt and pepper to taste

STEPS

  1. Step 1

    2. gather all ingredients

  2. Step 2

    3. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add potatoes and cook until tender but still firm, about 15 minutes; drain and chop.

  3. Step 3

    4. At the same time, place eggs in a saucepan and cover with cold water. Bring water to a boil; cover, remove from heat, and let eggs stand in hot water for 10 to 12 minutes. Remove from hot water; peel and chop.

  4. Step 4

    5. Combine cooked potatoes and eggs with mayonnaise, celery, relish, garlic, mustard, salt, and pepper in a large bowl.

  5. Step 5

    6. Gently mix together and serve warm.
